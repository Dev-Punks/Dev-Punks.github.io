Projects

Hackamon
Hackamon is a game where you have two goals, score as many points as you can and survive all five rounds. It rewards a tactical approach to gameplay as you manipulate the map by breaking down the walls around you and building them up to create the world you want to play in. How long can you survive?

Technologies: HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.


Space Zooey
Take zooey through space, kill aliens, and collect coins. As the game progresses, it will get harder to survive. Keep zooey alive for as long as possible => get the highest score to win & game over when aliens reach you.

Technologies: HTML, CSS, and Sprites.
